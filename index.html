LEHER

Steering Indian Agriculture to Net-Zero emissions
while making the transition economically profitable
for the farmer

Leher is a digitized platform that connects farmers with solar pump installer networks and financers in India in order to reduce the upfront payable cost for installing solar irrigation systems, increase the farmer’s agricultural output and profitability. We realize that rising diesel prices impact farmer incomes, despite electrical subsidies offered by the government.
